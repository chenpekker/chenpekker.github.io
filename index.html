Nice to meet you!

I'm currently a senior at HMC studying computer science with humanities concentrations in economics and religious studies. I love to learn and to travel, both of which I try to do as often as I can. I had never tried CS before my freshman year at Mudd because I always felt that it just wasn't the thing for me, despite loving and excelling in STEM my whole life. For this reason, I'm deeply passionate about breaking down stereotypes and expanding access to computer science education, specifically to women and other underrepresented minority groups in the field.
